App Logo Redesigns
App Logo Redesigns
I discovered that you could use different app icons on the iPhone. I never did like the block look of apps and thought it covered up to much of the screen. As a result, I decided I was going to redesign some of my favorite apps in a gradient style. The redesign not only gets rid of the block look and allows for more of the background to show through, it also allows to me to better categorize them based off of color. Click on the picture to discover all of the app redesigns.

Real World Objects
I decided that I was also going to redesign real world objects to make them not only look better, but also to add features and be more user friendly. This all started when I saw the new remote for the Amazon Firestick. I hated that there wasn't more programmable buttons. I also did not like the location of some of the buttons on the remote. So I redesigned it. In the future I would like to redesign more real world items. To see the firestick remote redesign click the picture.
