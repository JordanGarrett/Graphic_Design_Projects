Firestick Remote Redesign

I noticed on the new version of the firestick remote they added programmable buttons. So I decided that two buttons wasn't enough and I added 8 more. I had to get rid of the big buttons that would take you to a specified app such as Netflix to make room for more programmable buttons. However, since you can program the new buttons, you can accomplish the same thing. In the future I would also hope to design stickers that you could put on top on the programmable buttons so you could know what they do at a glance. Also I did not like where the live tv button was so I moved it right under the button that increases or decreases the channel number as they have a similar function. I found it weird that some firestick remotes had the picture in picture button and some did not. I added the button to the redesign right under the play button as that is what you would use most frequently after using picture in picture. Finally, I had to move the alexa button in line with the headphone button and power button. I think it actually looks better that way.
